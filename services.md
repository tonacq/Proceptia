# Services

Proceptia delivers engineering services aligned to manufacturing and process-based facilities, supporting both operational and project-based decisions.

Services include:

- Engineering analysis and numerical modelling
- Mass and energy balance development
- Process and plant concept evaluation
- Technical feasibility and options analysis
- Project definition and scope development
- Owner-side engineering advisory
- Project design support within manufacturing and process domains

Where design activities are undertaken, they form part of defined project engagements and are scoped to manufacturing and process requirements.
